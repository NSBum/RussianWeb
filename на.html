На


The preposition на roughly means "on". Like в, it can take either the accusative or the prepositional case depending on whether is motion involved.


на + accusative:


Here, the meaning is "on" but there is a direction towards something else. Imagine you are answering the question «Куда? На что?» "Where to? On what?"


Человек получил на велосипеде. (Тhe man got on the bicycle.)

Собака работает на полу (The dogs run on the floor.)

Кошка прыгает на стол. (The cat jumps on the table.)


на + prepositional:


Here, the meaning is "on" with a static position. You should imagine that you are answering the question «Где? На чём? На ком?» "Where? On what? On whom?"


Кошка лежит на стуле (A cat lies on the chair.)

Клещ на собаке. (A tick is on the dog.)

prepositional

The prepositional case is only used with prepositions as opposed to the accusative case which can be used with or without a preposition.


1. It can be used to designate a place.


Где ты был?   Where have you been?  

Я был в школе.  I have been at school.


2. It can designate something being talked or thought about.


Думать о семье.  To think about a family.

Говорить о погоде. To talk about the weather.

About


The New Penguin Russian Course (NPRC) is a frequently-used textbook for Russian self-study. This guide is intended to be an outline of the course material and supplementary resources that go along with the text. It is a work in-progress. I'm starting around chapter 20 which is where I am in the book. I'll try to fill in the gaps as I have time.

The preposition В has several meanings depending on the case of its object.


В + accusative:


If в is used with the accusative case, then it means "to" with motion toward something. Imagine you are answering the question «Куда?» "Where to?"


Машина едет в город.

Самолет летит в Москву.


В + prepositional:


If в is used with the prepositional case, then it means a static location. Imagine you are answering the question «Где?». 


Самолет в Москве

Мой гитара в такси

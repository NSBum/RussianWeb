There are three groups of Russian declensions.


First declension - mostly feminine nouns ending in -а or -я. Some masculine nouns with the same endings, e.g. дядя

Second declension - mostly masculine nouns with no ending or neuter nouns with -о or -е ending.

Third declension - feminine nouns with no ending in the nominative form, e.g. дочь

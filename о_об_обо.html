о/об/обо


The preposition о/об is a common preposition in the Russian language. It is pronounced as an unstressed vowel. It is writted as о typically; but as об before а, э, и, о, у. The form обо is used before certain clusters like мн- or вс-.


О/об takes the prepositional case where it means "concerning, of, on"


Examples:


- книга о России      a book on или about Russia


It can take the accusative case where is means "against"


Examples:


- волны бьются о скалы      the waves are beating against the cliffs
